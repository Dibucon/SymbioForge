# SymbioForge – How to start a local circle with paper vouchers

## What you need
- 5–30 people in your neighbourhood, town, or community
- Some paper, a pen, and a whiteboard / shared notebook
- Trust and curiosity (no fear of making mistakes)

## Step 1: Invitation
Invite people who are willing to run a simple experiment for 6 months. No pressure, no obligation.

## Step 2: The rules (print them or write them down)
1. **Voluntariness & Exit** – Anyone can leave, anytime.
2. **Demurrage** – 1% per month on balances above 1000 units (e.g., 1000 “Coins”).
3. **Basic Income** – Everyone receives 500 Coins at the start. After one month, another 500 Coins for everyone.
4. **Voting** – One person, one vote. Decisions are made after discussion by simple majority.
5. **Local autonomy** – Your circle decides which goods/services are offered in Coins.
6. **Fork** – If half the group wants new rules, you can form a separate circle.

## Step 3: First transactions
- Give everyone 500 Coins as paper vouchers (handwritten, with a signature).
- Demurrage: After one month, any voucher worth more than 1000 Coins is reduced by 1% (cross out old value, write new value).
- Exchange help: gardening, rides, vegetables, childcare, tutoring, repairs, etc.

## Step 4: Gather regularly
Meet once a week or month. Exchange vouchers, discuss what works and what doesn’t. Improve your rules together.

## Step 5: Share your experience
Document what worked and what failed. Open an Issue or a pull request on GitHub. Help other circles learn.

## Good luck! Remember: it’s just an experiment. If it fails, you lost nothing – except maybe a few hours of real neighbourliness.
