# SymbioForge – Constitution of Failure

1. **Every new rule must fit on one page and explain why existing rules are insufficient.**
2. **For each new rule, one old rule must be removed.**
3. **Annual external “Failure Audit”** – An outsider checks for power concentration, exclusion, abuse.
4. **Any circle can fork the protocol** – without guilt or permission.
5. **No personality cult** – No one may receive more than 10% of direct delegations (if delegation is used).
6. **If these rules are grossly violated**, the circle may dissolve itself (75% majority) and distribute its assets.

*This is not a constitution for eternal order – it’s a tool to enable correction.*
