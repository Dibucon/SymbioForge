# SymbioForge – A possible future (not a promise)

We do not have a master plan. We have a direction.

## In 1–2 years

- Dozens of local circles, each with 5–100 people, using paper vouchers or simple spreadsheets.
- A shared library of **real‑world experiments**: what worked, what failed, what surprised us.
- Translated core rules in 10+ languages.
- No central authority, no token, no blockchain – unless circles truly need it.

## In 5–10 years

- Some circles may choose to use **lightweight digital tools** (shared ledgers, optional apps) to reduce bookkeeping.
- A loose **federation of circles** that share open standards (like how email works) but keep local autonomy.
- Basic income funded partly by demurrage fees, partly by local production (energy, food, care).
- **No mandatory global governance.** Forking remains the main conflict resolution.

## What we will never become

- A global cryptocurrency with speculation and whales.
- An AI‑ruled society.
- A political party or a replacement for states.
- A system that forces anyone to participate.

> The future is not designed in advance. It is discovered through thousands of small, honest experiments.

**We are here to learn, not to convert.**
