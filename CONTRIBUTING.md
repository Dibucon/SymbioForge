# Contributing to SymbioForge

This is not a software project with pull requests and code reviews.  
It’s a **living protocol** for local cooperation.

## You can contribute by:

- **Starting a local circle** – use the 6 rules, paper vouchers, and share your experience.
- **Opening an Issue** – tell us what worked, what failed, what is unclear.
- **Translating** these documents into your language (add a link to your translation).
- **Improving clarity** – if a rule is hard to understand, suggest better wording.
- **Adding a local case study** – after 3–6 months, write a short “What we learned”.

## What we will NOT accept:

- Adding new global rules, exceptions, or complexity.
- Changing the 6 core rules (they are minimal by design).
- Turning this into a token, blockchain, or AI‑governed system.
