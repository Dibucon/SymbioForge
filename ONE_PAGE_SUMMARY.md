# SymbioForge – One‑Page Summary
A printable flyer for recruiting members

**We are 6 rules. Nothing more.**

1. **Voluntary** – come and go freely.
2. **Demurrage 1%/month** – money flows, does not hoard.
3. **Basic income** – everyone gets the same monthly grant.
4. **One person, one vote** – no scores, no bonuses.
5. **Local circles** – you decide your own projects.
6. **Fork anytime** – disagree? Start your own circle.

**No blockchain. No app. No AI. No fees.**

Start with 5 friends, paper vouchers, and a monthly meeting.
