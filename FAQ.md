# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does SymbioForge need blockchain, crypto, or an app?**  
A: No. Paper vouchers and a notebook are enough. Technology is optional.

**Q: How is the basic income funded?**  
A: In a small circle, members can seed it with their own resources (time, goods, small donations). Over time, demurrage fees (1% on large balances) flow back into the UBI pool.

**Q: What stops people from taking advantage of the system?**  
A: The UBI is modest. Free riding is possible but not catastrophic. If a circle finds it unsustainable, they can change rules, fork, or dissolve.

**Q: Is this political?**  
A: No. It is a voluntary tool for neighbours, not a replacement for governments or markets.

**Q: Can I use real money (Euro, Dollar, etc.) alongside SymbioCoins?**  
A: Yes. Circles decide how they handle exchange. Many start as pure complementary currency.

**Q: What if my circle grows to 100 people?**  
A: Keep the same simple rules. If complexity increases, split into multiple circles (forking). That is a feature, not a bug.
