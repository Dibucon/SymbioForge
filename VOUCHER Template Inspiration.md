
# Voucher templates – inspiration only

The voucher sheets on this page are just **one examples** of how your local circle's paper vouchers could look.

Feel free to:
- Use it as-is (print, cut, stamp, write serial numbers).
- Modify the design (add your circle's name, change colours, add a background).
- Create your own from scratch – as long as you keep the six core rules visible somewhere (demurrage, voluntary, forkable).

**Your vouchers do not need to be perfect.** Hand‑drawn on recycled paper is fine. The only essential elements are:
- Denomination (e.g., 20 units)
- Serial number (to prevent counterfeiting)
- Circle name or stamp (to show which community issued them)
